Summary of The 10 Most Common Web Design Mistakes

Web design is a field where many things have to be taken into consideration. These range from pretty design to usability. Following good guidelines will help your website to become more attractive to your end-users.

Good design and well-working implementation

Users judge a website based on how good it looks as well as how well it works according to them. Pretty design indicates trustworthiness. However, a website can lose its trustworhiness by not functioning as expected or by having its critical functions not work well. Non-well functioning sites are seen as suspicious despite being legit.

The significance of speed

Many Internet users have grown accustomed to being able to consume content rapidly. This means that web developers have to take this into consideration. If you want people to stay on your site or to come to it in the first place, it has to load rapidly. It cannot take more than 5 seconds to load the site because that's the limit after which your users leave.

The importance of resposivity

Nowadays, almost everyone uses Internet on their phones. This means that web developers have to cater to mobile devices as well. This means that websites are made in a way that they automatically scale based on the size of the screen. When screen size shrinks, so does the website to fit it.

SEO optimization

Weak SEO can do harm to your website's visibility. This is because when SEO is low, the search engines of the Internet won't display it within the first search results of a search results page. However, there are ways to try to improve it. For example, you can add keywords to the pages of your website. This will tell the search engines what the page is about. After this, they can in return raise your site higher in the ranking when they how well it matches the desired search results of a search engine user.
